# Launch Prompts

These are copy-paste prompts for people trying the repo for the first time in Claude, Codex, Cursor, or similar tools.

## Prompt 1: General Start

Use this repository as a compliance runtime. Start with `START-HERE.md`, ask me the minimum questions needed to understand my AI use case, then route me to the right workflow and produce a source-backed analysis.

## Prompt 2: AI Act Classification

Use `START-HERE.md` and run the AI Act classification path for my use case. Ask only the questions needed to determine likely classification, role, obligations, and immediate next actions. Output a source-backed analysis and a decision record.

## Prompt 3: GDPR And DPIA Screen

Use this repo to assess GDPR lawful basis, Article 9 issues, Article 22 concerns, and whether a DPIA is likely required. Start with the relevant questionnaire, state assumptions explicitly, and output a source-backed analysis with escalation points.

## Prompt 4: Vendor Review

Use this repo to run a structured compliance and trust review of an external AI vendor. Ask me the vendor due-diligence questions first, then produce a source-backed analysis with a go, conditional-go, or no-go view.

## Prompt 5: Recruitment AI

Use `START-HERE.md` in this repo. I need to assess an AI recruitment or candidate-screening tool. Ask me the minimum questions needed to classify it under the EU AI Act, assess GDPR lawful basis and Article 22 concerns, and screen whether a DPIA is likely required. Output a source-backed analysis and a short decision record.

## Prompt 6: Public-Sector Chatbot

Use `START-HERE.md` in this repo. I need to assess a public-sector chatbot or citizen-facing assistant. Ask the right intake questions, classify the use case, screen the GDPR and DPIA implications, and give me a source-backed analysis plus a decision record.
