# Deployment Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist before pilot launch, wider rollout, or production release of an AI system.

## System Understanding

- [ ] Intended purpose is documented.
- [ ] Known limitations and failure modes are documented.
- [ ] Human review or override process is defined where needed.

## AI Act And GDPR Foundations

- [ ] AI Act classification is recorded.
- [ ] Organisation role is recorded.
- [ ] GDPR lawful basis is documented by processing stage.
- [ ] Article 9 conditions are documented where relevant.
- [ ] Article 22 assessment is documented where relevant.

## DPIA And Risk

- [ ] DPIA is completed or a justified no-DPIA decision is recorded.
- [ ] Key risks to individuals are documented.
- [ ] Residual risk owner is assigned.
- [ ] Escalation route is defined for high-impact issues.

## Data And Operations

- [ ] Data categories and sources are documented.
- [ ] Retention position is defined.
- [ ] International transfer implications are reviewed.
- [ ] Monitoring, logging, and review responsibilities are assigned.

## User-Facing Controls

- [ ] Required transparency messaging is prepared.
- [ ] User complaints or challenge routes are defined where relevant.
- [ ] Internal teams know when not to rely on the system.

## Release Decision

- [ ] Go
- [ ] Conditional go
- [ ] No-go

## Notes

Document blockers, missing approvals, and mandatory post-launch follow-ups.
