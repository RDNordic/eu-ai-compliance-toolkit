# EU AI Act Application Dates

> Last updated: 2026-02-17
>
> This page is a practical timeline summary of Article 113 of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689. Always verify against the official legal text and current guidance.

---

## Core Timeline

| Date | Applies From | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| 2024-08-01 | Entry into force | AI Act entered into force 20 days after publication in the Official Journal. |
| 2025-02-02 | Chapter I and Chapter II | Includes AI literacy (Art. 4) and prohibited practices (Art. 5). |
| 2025-08-02 | Chapter III Section 4, Chapter V, Chapter VII, Chapter XII, and Art. 78 | Includes GPAI obligations and notified body framework elements. |
| 2026-08-02 | General application date for most obligations | Includes high-risk AI system obligations and transparency obligations under Art. 50, unless a later date applies. |
| 2027-08-02 | Article 6(1) systems | High-risk AI systems that are safety components of products (or are products) under Annex I legislation with third-party conformity assessment. |
| 2030-12-31 | Legacy large-scale IT systems | Specific transition deadline for certain Annex X large-scale EU IT systems placed on the market or put into service before 2027-08-02. |

---

## How to Use This Timeline

1. Confirm whether your use case is an AI system, GPAI model, or both.
2. Determine your role: provider, deployer, importer, distributor, authorised representative.
3. Check if Article 6(1) or Annex X transitional rules apply.
4. Record the date logic in your claim-to-source register.
