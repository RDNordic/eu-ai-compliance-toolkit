# Questionnaire: Recruitment AI

Use this questionnaire when the AI system supports hiring, candidate screening, ranking, interview analysis, or workforce decisions.

## Questions

- What stage of recruitment or employment does the system affect
- Does it screen out or rank candidates
- Does it evaluate CVs, tests, interviews, video, audio, or behavioral signals
- Does it infer traits, personality, emotion, or suitability
- Is any biometric, health, diversity, or other special-category data involved
- Can a human meaningfully override the output before a decision is made
- What practical effect does the output have on the person
- Are candidates informed that AI is being used
- What lawful basis is currently assumed
- What safeguards or review steps exist today
- Is the system developed internally or provided by a vendor

## Why This Matters

Recruitment and employment contexts can raise heightened AI Act and GDPR concerns, especially around:

- significant effects on individuals
- weak consent assumptions in employment contexts
- Article 22 questions
- special-category data risk
