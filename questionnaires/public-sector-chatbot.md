# Questionnaire: Public-Sector Chatbot

Use this questionnaire for public-sector assistants, service chatbots, triage bots, or citizen-facing information tools.

## Questions

- Which public body or service area is involved
- What is the chatbot expected to help users do
- Does it only provide information, or can it influence access to rights, benefits, services, or complaints handling
- Are children or vulnerable groups likely users
- What personal data is collected or inferred
- Does the tool authenticate users or access case data
- Does it route, prioritize, or recommend decisions to staff
- Can staff review and override outputs
- Is the system built in-house or supplied by a vendor
- What records or logs are retained
- Are there accessibility or language obligations in scope
