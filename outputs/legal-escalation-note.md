# Output Template: Legal Escalation Note

Use this template when there are unresolved issues that should be reviewed by legal counsel, DPO functions, or formal governance bodies.

## Fields

- Issue requiring escalation
- Why the issue matters
- Facts confirmed
- Assumptions currently being made
- Main legal or regulatory uncertainty
- Potential impact if assumption is wrong
- Sources consulted
- Specific question for counsel or governance review
- Decision needed by when

## Trigger Rule

Use this output when the assistant cannot responsibly close the issue with high confidence from the available facts and sources.
