# Privacy

## Core Point

Cloning this repository is not the same thing as sending data to an AI provider.

The repo itself is local content. Privacy risk arises when a user inputs project data, personal data, or confidential material into an AI assistant that may process, retain, or transmit that data under the provider’s own terms and configuration.

## Do Not Assume

Do not assume that using an AI tool with this repo means:

- your prompts stay only on your machine
- provider-side retention is disabled
- training on inputs is disabled
- subprocessors are absent
- international transfers are not involved

Those questions depend on the specific tool, account tier, tenant settings, and vendor contract.

## Safe Default

Before using this repo with a real use case:

- remove direct identifiers where possible
- avoid raw special-category data
- avoid production secrets
- avoid confidential contracts unless approved
- prefer summarized fact patterns over document dumps

## For Regulated Or Public-Sector Users

You should review:

- internal policy on AI tool use
- procurement and DPA status for the chosen AI provider
- retention and training settings
- access controls and logging
- whether the use case itself requires a DPIA or legal review

## Honest Boundary

This repo can help teams ask the right privacy questions. It does not itself guarantee that the AI tool in use handles data appropriately.
